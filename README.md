## üîí Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 9, 2025

### 1. Information We Collect

#### 1.1 Prayer Notifications Preferences
- **Purpose:** To send you timely prayer reminders
- **Type:** Your preferred notification settings and times
- **Storage:** Stored locally on your device
- **Sharing:** Not shared with any third parties

### 2. What We DON'T Collect

‚úÖ We do NOT collect:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data or GPS coordinates
- Your prayer history or religious practices
- Contact lists or photos
- Any data that can identify you personally

### 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:
- Display prayer times for your selected location
- Send prayer time notifications based on your preferences

### 4. Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** All personal preferences are stored locally on your device
- **No Cloud Sync:** Your data is not synchronized to any cloud servers
- **Encryption:** Device-level encryption through iOS security features
- **No Accounts:** No registration or account creation required

### 5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use the following third-party services:

#### 5.1 Apple Services
- **Local Notifications:** For prayer time alerts
- **Privacy:** Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

#### 5.2 Prayer Time Calculation
- We use pre-calculated prayer times for specific locations
- No data is transmitted to external services
- All calculations are performed locally on your device

### 6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for users of all ages who wish to track prayer times.

### 7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Choose Your Location:** Manually select your city/region from a list
- **Disable Notifications:** Through iOS Settings
- **Delete App Data:** By uninstalling the application

### 8. Data Retention

- No location data is collected or retained
- Preferences are stored until you uninstall the app
- No data is retained on external servers

### 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy in this repository
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Displaying a notice in the app (for significant changes)
